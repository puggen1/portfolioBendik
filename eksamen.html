About the project

The exam for my first year of studying. I where to make a blog, i could choose the theme myself, so i chose racing.

I had to design and implement an blog website, that retrives its content from an external server.

Planning was not a required part of the assignment, but i choose to make a detailed plan at the start, as it is important

The requirements

  • Headless CMS where alle the data was stored.
  • the data had to be retrieved and displayed.
  • The front page needed a content carousel / slider.
  • some images should go full screen when clicked.
  • A contact form that validates with javascript.
  • optional: diffrent filtering systems
